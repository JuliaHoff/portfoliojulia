Coty

Coty is one of the world’s leading beauty companies with approximately $9 billion in revenue, a rich entrepreneurial heritage and an iconic portfolio of leading brands. Coty are the global leader in fragrances, with the number two position in salon hair and number three in color cosmetics.

Brief: How can Coty bring Millenials to do more hairdressing services (ideally into salons).

How: Did research and maped millennials behaviours. And came up with a campaign based on the fact that millanials are the DYI (do it yourself) generation. We came up with idea of a DYI salon, where people could go and experiment with friends on cutting and dying their hair in an controlled environment, so if something goes wrong they have a professional on an arms length to come to their rescue.

Skills: Create a strategy and concept and backing it up with data.

Médecins Sans Frontières

Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) is an international, independent, medical humanitarian organisation that delivers emergency aid to people affected by armed conflict, epidemics, natural disasters and exclusion from healthcare. MSF offers assistance to people based on need, irrespective of race, religion, gender or political affiliation.

Brief: In a rapidly changing medial and political climate it is hard to stand out and get attention. How can we make MSF engage and build long-term empathic, relationships with potential donors and supporters?Budget of 1million SEK.

How: We created the concept called Reality Check, a direct advertising campaign targeting donors and potential donors by sending out 100 000 cardboard VR headsets and creating a campaign website were the people could watch 360 VR films from locations where MSF is currently operating. The goal with the campaign was to make it go viral and help bring attention and raise awareness to MSF’s work and ongoing crisis.

Skills: Research, Strategy, Creative thinking, writing script for the case movie

Distrikstandvården

Distriktstandvården (District Dental) is a private dental company that operates 18 clinics in the county. Started in 2010 when Folktandvården, which is in the public sector, sold 10 clinics that became Distrikstandvården. Since then, they have expanded and today they have over 250 employees. They stand for a modern, dedicated, trustworthy and available dental care.

Brief: Increase the number of bookings on distrikstandvården.se’s website and suggest relevant improvement for optimising there website.

How: Analysed the website using Google Analytics, suggest they use HotJar and help them implement it on the site. Looked at the data and did user tests to create hypotheses on what to improve on the website in order to make more people book an appointment.

Skills: Google Analytics, HotJar, User Tests, Google Data Studio
